引言

欢迎使用「三毛钱」（以下简称「本应用」）。我们重视您的隐私，并致力于以透明、负责的方式处理相关信息。 本隐私政策说明本应用在使用过程中可能涉及的信息处理方式。

本应用是一款本地视频播放工具，主要功能为播放应用内置视频内容，并提供语言偏好设置。 我们不会要求您注册账号，也不会主动收集可用于识别您个人身份的敏感信息。

我们收集的信息

本应用不收集以下类型的个人信息：

  • 姓名、电话号码、电子邮件地址等身份信息
  • 精确或粗略地理位置信息
  • 通讯录、照片、相机、麦克风等内容
  • 设备唯一标识符用于广告追踪
  • 支付或财务信息

本应用仅在您的设备本地存储以下非敏感偏好设置，用于记住您的使用选择：

  • 语言偏好：例如「跟随系统」「中文」或「English」

上述信息通过设备本地存储机制（如 iOS 的 UserDefaults / Android 的 SharedPreferences）保存， 仅用于在本设备上恢复您的设置，不会上传至我们的服务器。

信息的使用方式

我们仅在以下目的范围内使用本地存储的信息：

  • 根据您的语言偏好展示界面文字
  • 根据您的语言偏好选择播放对应的内置视频资源
  • 在您下次打开应用时恢复上述设置

网络与第三方服务

本应用的核心功能可在无网络连接的情况下使用。应用不会主动向第三方服务器发送您的个人信息。

本应用当前未集成以下服务：

  • 广告与分析统计 SDK
  • 社交账号登录
  • 云端同步或远程账户系统
  • 第三方支付服务

若未来版本新增网络功能或第三方服务，我们将更新本隐私政策，并在必要时通过应用内提示或其他适当方式告知您。

数据存储与安全

语言偏好数据仅保存在您的设备本地。卸载本应用后，相关本地数据通常会被系统一并删除。 我们采取合理措施，确保应用本身不滥用或超出上述范围处理信息。

儿童隐私

本应用不面向 13 周岁以下儿童主动收集个人信息。若您认为我们在未经监护人同意的情况下处理了儿童相关信息， 请通过下方联系方式与我们取得联系，我们将尽快核实并处理。

您的权利

您可以通过以下方式管理相关信息：

  • 在应用内「设置」中随时更改语言偏好
  • 通过卸载本应用删除设备上的本地偏好数据
  • 通过邮件联系我们，咨询与本隐私政策相关的问题

政策更新

我们可能不时更新本隐私政策。更新后的版本将发布在本页面，并修改文首「生效日期」。 若变更涉及重要权利或义务，我们将通过合理方式提醒您查阅最新版本。

English

Introduction

Welcome to "San Mao Qian" (the "App"). We respect your privacy and are committed to handling information in a transparent and responsible manner. This Privacy Policy explains how the App processes information during use.

The App is a local video playback tool that plays built-in video content and allows you to set language preferences. We do not require account registration and do not actively collect sensitive personal information that can identify you.

Information We Collect

The App does not collect the following types of personal information:

  • Identity information such as name, phone number, or email address
  • Precise or coarse location data
  • Contacts, photos, camera, microphone, or similar content
  • Device identifiers for advertising tracking
  • Payment or financial information

The App stores only the following non-sensitive preference locally on your device:

  • Language preference, such as "Follow System", "Chinese", or "English"

This information is saved using local device storage (such as UserDefaults on iOS or SharedPreferences on Android) and is used only to restore your settings on the same device. It is not uploaded to our servers.

How We Use Information

We use locally stored information only for the following purposes:

  • Displaying interface text according to your language preference
  • Selecting the corresponding built-in video resource to play
  • Restoring your settings the next time you open the App

Network and Third-Party Services

The core features of the App can be used without an internet connection. The App does not actively transmit your personal information to third-party servers.

The App currently does not integrate:

  • Advertising or analytics SDKs
  • Social account login
  • Cloud sync or remote account systems
  • Third-party payment services

If future versions add network features or third-party services, we will update this Privacy Policy and notify you when appropriate.

Data Storage and Security

Language preference data is stored only on your device. Uninstalling the App will typically remove related local data. We take reasonable measures to ensure the App does not misuse or process information beyond the scope described above.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have processed children's information without parental consent, please contact us using the details below.

Your Rights

You can manage related information by:

  • Changing language preferences in the App's Settings at any time
  • Deleting local preference data by uninstalling the App
  • Contacting us by email with questions about this Privacy Policy

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page with a revised effective date. If changes materially affect your rights or obligations, we will provide reasonable notice.

Contact Us

If you have any questions, comments, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

We will respond to your inquiry within a reasonable time.
