简介 · Short description
以离线为主的数独应用：内置完整解题引擎与多层提示（挑战、每日挑战、训练与教程等）；支持 PDF 导出与扫码导入；可选本地资料与轻度「修为/境界」包装。无需强制联网账号。
Offline-first Sudoku with a full solving engine and multi-layer hints (challenge, daily challenges, training, tutorials). PDF export and QR puzzle import. Optional local profile and light cultivation-themed progression. No required online account.

English

This application is designed to work primarily offline. It does not require an account, does not show third-party ads in this build, and does not collect analytics or tracking identifiers for profiling.

Data you create (such as saved games, settings, training progress, and optional local profile fields) is stored locally on your device. Export/import features use files you choose and are under your control.

The camera and photo library are used when you scan a puzzle QR code (live camera or choosing an image). QR decoding runs on your device.

When you set a profile avatar, you pick an image with the system file or media picker; the image is saved only in the app’s local storage on your device.

The app does not upload your photos, avatar images, or puzzle images to us for these features.

If you use features that open external links or system services (for example sharing, printing, or export destinations you choose), those services are governed by their own providers’ policies.

This page summarizes how the current build handles data on your device. If you distribute the app through app stores, ensure your store privacy questionnaire and disclosures match this behavior.
